Getting Started Guide

Follow these steps to implement a data-driven campaign strategy

1 Establish Your Base Metrics

Start by calculating your win number (votes needed to win), target universe size, and contact goals. Use our Campaign Metrics Calculator to quickly determine these essential figures based on your district demographics and historical turnout data.

2 Develop Your Voter Targeting Plan

Identify your base supporters, persuadable voters, and unlikely supporters. Prioritize your outreach based on both support level and likelihood of voting. Focus resources on high-impact segments where your campaign can make the most difference.

3 Create Your Campaign Timeline

Map out key campaign phases and activities using our Campaign Timeline Template. Work backward from election day to establish critical deadlines, messaging shifts, and resource allocation changes throughout the campaign.

4 Design Your Field Operation

Use our Field Operation Checklist to build a comprehensive volunteer recruitment and management system. Establish clear roles, training procedures, and accountability metrics to ensure your ground game operates efficiently.

5 Implement Multi-Channel Communications

Deploy our Messaging Framework Template to ensure consistent communication across all channels. Customize your message for different voter segments while maintaining your core campaign narrative.

6 Monitor Performance & Adapt

Use our Campaign KPI Dashboard to track progress toward your goals. Establish regular review intervals to assess what's working and what needs adjustment, allowing your campaign to respond quickly to changing conditions.
