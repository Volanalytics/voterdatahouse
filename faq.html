Our Services

What services does Voter Data House offer?

We offer a comprehensive suite of data-driven campaign services including:

  • All-In-One Tech - Campaign website, communication platforms, and integrated systems
  • NextGen Campaign Operations - AI-powered campaign automation and gamification
  • District Mapping & Field Strategy - Voter targeting and field operation optimization
  • Performance Tracking - Real-time analytics and campaign monitoring

Each service can be customized to fit your campaign's unique needs and budget.

How much do your services cost?

Our service pricing varies based on campaign size, service package, and specific needs. We offer solutions for campaigns of all sizes, from local races to statewide campaigns.

We believe in transparent pricing and offer a free initial consultation to understand your needs and provide a detailed proposal. We structure our packages to align with typical campaign budgets at each level.

Contact us for a custom quote tailored to your specific campaign requirements.

Do you work with campaigns of all sizes?

Yes! We specifically designed our services to be accessible to campaigns of all sizes. We have experience working with:

  • Local races (City Council, School Board, etc.)
  • County and municipal elections
  • State legislative campaigns
  • Congressional and statewide races

Our scalable approach allows us to right-size our services to match your campaign's scope and budget while still providing the data-driven edge that can make the difference in tight races.

Do you work with both Democratic and Republican campaigns?

We believe in the democratic process and the importance of data-driven campaigns regardless of party affiliation. Our services are available to candidates from all major parties who are committed to ethical campaigning.

To maintain the highest level of service and avoid conflicts of interest, we do not work with multiple campaigns competing for the same office in the same election cycle.

What technology platforms do you use?

Our system integrates with major CRM platforms, voter file systems, and digital advertising platforms to create a unified campaign technology ecosystem.
