Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about VoterDataHouse

What makes VoterDataHouse different from other campaign communication platforms?

VoterDataHouse is the only platform specifically designed for political campaigns that combines true omnichannel capabilities with AI-powered campaign staff assistance. While other platforms may offer multiple communication channels, ours is unique in providing seamless transitions between channels with full context preservation, voter journey customization, and real-time analytics across all touchpoints.

How long does it take to implement VoterDataHouse for my campaign?

Basic implementation can be completed in as little as 2-3 weeks, with more complex setups taking 4-6 weeks. Our onboarding team works closely with your campaign to ensure a smooth transition. We offer expedited implementation options for campaigns with urgent needs, especially during election season.

Can VoterDataHouse integrate with our existing CRM and voter database?

Yes, VoterDataHouse is built on an open API architecture specifically designed to integrate with popular campaign tools. We offer pre-built connectors for major CRM systems like NGP VAN, NationBuilder, and Salesforce, as well as custom API integrations for proprietary systems. Our integration specialists can help determine the best approach for your specific technology stack.

What kind of training and support do you provide?

We offer comprehensive training for all user roles, including admin, supervisor, and staff levels. Our support includes:

  • Initial onboarding and platform training
  • On-demand video tutorials and documentation
  • Dedicated customer success manager
  • 24/7 technical support via phone, email, and chat
  • Regular strategy sessions to optimize your campaign's use of the platform

Is VoterDataHouse compliant with campaign finance regulations?

Yes, VoterDataHouse is designed with campaign compliance in mind. Our platform includes features to help maintain compliance with FEC regulations, TCPA requirements for political calls and texts, and state-specific campaign laws. We provide regular updates to keep the system current with changing regulations and offer optional compliance training for your team.

Can we customize the system for our specific campaign needs?

Absolutely. VoterDataHouse offers extensive customization options, including:

  • Tailored voter journeys and communication flows
  • Custom reporting dashboards
  • Personalized voter self-service options
  • Branded campaign interfaces and communications
  • Adjustable automation rules and routing logic

Our implementation team works closely with you to configure the platform according to your campaign's specific strategy and needs.
