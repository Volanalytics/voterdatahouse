Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can we get up and running with the platform?

Most campaigns are fully operational within 1-2 weeks. Our onboarding team provides comprehensive training and setup support to ensure a smooth transition.

Can we integrate existing voter files and databases?

Yes, our platform is designed to integrate with standard voter file formats, NGP VAN, and most major CRM systems. Our data team will assist with mapping and importing your existing data.

Is the platform suitable for local campaigns with limited budgets?

Absolutely. Our Essentials plan is specifically designed for local races with focused needs and smaller teams. We also offer special pricing for down-ballot campaigns.

How secure is voter data on your platform?

Security is our top priority. We implement end-to-end encryption, role-based access controls, comprehensive audit logs, and regular security assessments. Our platform complies with all relevant data protection regulations.

Can we use the platform for multiple races or campaigns?

Yes, our Enterprise plan supports multi-race management with appropriate data segregation and permissions, making it ideal for consulting firms, party committees, and organizations running multiple campaigns.
