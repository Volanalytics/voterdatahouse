What Is GOTV Mapping System?

GOTV Mapping System is a comprehensive voter outreach platform that integrates Google Maps with voter data management to create a powerful Get Out The Vote (GOTV) and canvassing tool. The system allows campaigns to visualize voter data geographically, plan efficient outreach routes, and track progress in real-time.

Accessible on both desktop and mobile devices, the platform includes interactive mapping, optimized routing, real-time data synchronization, team management, and detailed analytics.

In addition to these core mapping capabilities, GOTV Mapping System features advanced workforce management tools, geocoding functionality, mobile field operations support, and customizable reporting dashboards.

Because the system is built on Google's reliable infrastructure with an open API architecture, campaigns can further customize their implementation by integrating with third-party tools like NGP VAN, PDI, or custom voter file systems.

Pricing & Plans Overview

GOTV Mapping System offers 3 pricing plans from $499 to $1,999 per campaign, providing options for organizations of all sizes:

  • Basic ($499 per campaign): Perfect for local campaigns with up to 5,000 voters and 5 user accounts. Includes Google Maps integration, basic geocoding, simple routing, email support, and 30-day access.
  • Standard ($999 per campaign): Ideal for district-level campaigns with up to 25,000 voters and 15 user accounts. Includes advanced analytics, optimized routing, team management, priority support, 90-day access, and custom team training.
  • Premium ($1,999 per campaign): For large-scale operations with unlimited voters and users. Includes custom integrations, dedicated support, full campaign cycle access, advanced security features, and custom reporting.

GOTV Mapping System Key Features

Interactive Mapping

GOTV Mapping System provides a dynamic visualization of your voter database directly on Google Maps, giving field teams an intuitive way to plan and execute outreach efforts:

  • Color-coded voter pins based on contact status (Not Contacted, Attempted Contact, Contacted, Will Vote, etc.)
  • Customizable filters for sorting voters by party, status, priority, and other variables
  • Detailed voter information accessible with a single click
  • Real-time updates as canvassers make contacts in the field

Geocoding Capabilities

Convert voter addresses into precise map coordinates with the system's built-in geocoding functionality:

Geocoding tools:

  • Batch Processing: Upload thousands of voter addresses and automatically convert them to map coordinates
  • Address Verification: Identify and flag potentially incorrect addresses before field deployment
  • Enhanced Location Data: Add building entrance coordinates, access codes, and parking information for apartments and complex buildings

Optimized Routing

Generate the most efficient canvassing routes to maximize voter contacts and minimize travel time:

The system analyzes voter locations, priority status, and team capacity to create optimal walking or driving routes for each canvasser. Routes can be customized based on transportation method, time constraints, and voter prioritization.

Routing features include:

  • Multi-Modal Routes: Create routes optimized for walking, driving, or public transportation
  • Priority-Based Sequencing: Ensure high-priority voters are contacted first
  • Time-Based Planning: Generate routes based on available canvassing hours
  • Turn-by-Turn Directions: Provide canvassers with detailed navigation instructions
  • Route Sharing: Easily share routes with team members via email or mobile app

Mobile Field Operations

The mobile-optimized interface ensures field teams can access all essential functions while canvassing:

  • Offline capabilities for areas with limited connectivity
  • Real-time location tracking to keep canvassers on route
  • Quick voter status updates with customizable notes
  • Photo and document upload capabilities for capturing relevant information

Mobile tools include:

  • Progressive Web App: Works on any device with a modern web browser, no app store download required
  • Battery Optimization: Reduced GPS polling frequency when stationary and dark mode for OLED screens
  • Weather Adaptation: Integration with weather forecasts to suggest indoor locations during extreme conditions

Data Synchronization

Keep all team members updated with real-time data syncing between canvassers and campaign headquarters:

As canvassers update voter contact information in the field, changes are immediately visible to all team members and campaign leadership, providing up-to-the-minute insights into progress and voter sentiment.

Synchronization capabilities:

  • Incremental Updates: Efficiently synchronize only changed data to minimize bandwidth usage
  • Conflict Resolution: Smart handling of simultaneous edits to prevent data loss
  • Offline Mode: Continue working without connectivity and sync automatically when connection is restored
  • Data Integrity: Regular checks to ensure data quality and consistency

Analytics and Reporting

Gain actionable insights with comprehensive analytics dashboards and reports:

Analytics capabilities include:

  • Real-Time Metrics: Track contacts, commitments, and other key performance indicators as they happen
  • Canvasser Performance: Evaluate individual and team efficiency with metrics like contacts per hour and conversion rates
  • Geographic Analysis: Identify areas with high response rates or coverage gaps
  • Trend Analysis: Monitor changes in voter sentiment and support over time
  • Custom Reports: Generate tailored reports for different stakeholders and campaign needs

Team Management

Efficiently organize and support your field team with robust management tools:

Team management features:

  • Role-Based Access: Assign appropriate permissions to administrators, field coordinators, and canvassers
  • Team Communication: Built-in messaging system for quick coordination and updates
  • Assignment Management: Create, distribute, and track daily canvassing assignments
  • Performance Tracking: Monitor team productivity and identify training opportunities
  • Scheduling Tools: Coordinate team availability and optimize field coverage

The Benefits of GOTV Mapping System

Here's how your campaign can benefit from implementing the GOTV Mapping System:

  • Increased canvassing efficiency
  • Data-driven decision making
  • Improved volunteer experience
  • Enhanced voter engagement

Increased Canvassing Efficiency

The optimized routing and real-time mapping capabilities dramatically improve the efficiency of your canvassing operation. Campaigns using GOTV Mapping System typically report 30-40% increases in the number of voter contacts per hour compared to traditional paper-based methods.

By eliminating wasted travel time and providing clear navigation, canvassers can focus their energy on quality voter interactions rather than finding their next address.

Data-Driven Decision Making

Comprehensive analytics provide campaign leadership with the insights needed to make informed strategic decisions. The system's reporting capabilities allow you to identify which neighborhoods have the highest support levels, which messages are most effective, and where to focus limited resources for maximum impact.

This real-time intelligence enables campaigns to quickly adapt strategies based on actual field data rather than intuition or outdated information.

Improved Volunteer Experience

The intuitive mobile interface and clear routing instructions make volunteering a more positive experience, increasing retention and participation. Volunteers appreciate having the tools they need to be successful and the ability to see their impact through the system's tracking metrics.

The reduction in administrative overhead also means campaign staff can focus on supporting volunteers rather than managing paperwork.

Enhanced Voter Engagement

By providing canvassers with detailed voter information at their fingertips, the system enables more personalized and effective voter interactions. Canvassers can reference previous contacts, stated issues of concern, and other relevant data to tailor their approach to each voter.

This level of personalization leads to higher conversion rates and stronger voter relationships throughout the campaign cycle.
